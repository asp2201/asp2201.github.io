I served as an **igneous petrologist** on the scientific party for **IODP Expedition 398** (Hellenic Arc Volcanic Field). Drill cores enable quantitative reconstructions of eruption magnitude, transport processes, and volcanic evolution at Santorini and nearby basins.

**Related papers:**
